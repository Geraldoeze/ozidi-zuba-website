# User Guide - Ozidi Zuba in Kubwa Website

Welcome! This guide explains how to use the new features added to your website.

## 🌙/☀️ Theme Switcher (Light/Dark Mode)

### What is Theme Switching?

The website automatically adapts to your preference for light or dark colors. This makes the site comfortable to view in any lighting condition and reduces eye strain.

### How to Use It

**Step 1**: Look for the moon/sun icon in the top navigation bar (next to the WhatsApp button)

**Step 2**: Click the icon to see the theme menu

**Step 3**: Choose your preferred theme:
- **Light Mode** ☀️ - White background with dark text
- **Dark Mode** 🌙 - Dark background with light text  
- **System** 💻 - Automatically matches your device's setting

**Step 4**: Your choice is automatically saved! The site will remember your preference next time you visit.

### Theme Features

✓ **Automatic Detection** - If you choose "System", the site automatically detects if your device is in dark mode

✓ **Instant Switching** - Theme changes immediately without refreshing the page

✓ **Persistent** - Your preference is remembered across all pages and future visits

✓ **Beautiful Design** - Both light and dark modes are professionally designed

✓ **Works Everywhere** - Theme applies to all pages (Home, About, Products, Gallery, Contact)

## 🖼️ Gallery Page

### What is the Gallery?

The Gallery is a showcase of Ozidi Zuba in Kubwa's motor spare parts and car maintenance products. It displays beautiful product photos with direct links to social media posts.

### How to Access the Gallery

**Method 1: From Navigation**
1. Look for "Gallery" in the main menu at the top
2. Click it to view the product showcase

**Method 2: From Home Page**
1. Scroll down the home page
2. Click on product cards or gallery links

### What You'll See in the Gallery

The gallery displays:
- 8 product categories (Engine Parts, Brakes, Suspension, etc.)
- Beautiful product photos
- Description of each product category
- Number of likes and comments (engagement metrics)
- Platform badge (Instagram or Facebook)

### Viewing Full Posts

To see the complete post on social media:

1. **Hover over any product card** (on desktop)
2. Look for action buttons
3. Click **"View Post"** button
4. You'll be taken to the full post on Instagram or Facebook
5. See detailed information, comments, and engagement

**On Mobile**: Just tap the card to see the "View Post" button

### Following on Social Media

At the bottom of the gallery page, you'll find:

- **Follow on Instagram** button - Click to visit the Instagram profile
- **Follow on Facebook** button - Click to visit the Facebook page

This lets you stay updated with the latest products and announcements!

### Gallery Features

✓ **Responsive Design** - Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile

✓ **Beautiful Hover Effects** - Smooth animations when you hover over products

✓ **Platform Badges** - Know which platform each post is from (Instagram/Facebook)

✓ **Engagement Metrics** - See how many people liked or commented on posts

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I go back to my preferred theme if I change it by mistake?
**A**: Just click the theme switcher again and select your preferred theme.

### Q: Will my theme preference be saved if I clear my browser cache?
**A**: Theme preference is stored locally and usually survives cache clearing. If it resets, just select your preference again.

### Q: Why is the gallery showing placeholder images?
**A**: The gallery will be updated with real product photos from Instagram/Facebook. Check back for updates!

### Q: Can I view full posts without leaving the website?
**A**: The website links to full posts on Instagram/Facebook. You can view them there and easily come back to the website.

### Q: Which social media should I follow for updates?
**A**: Follow both Instagram (@zuba_in_kubwa) and Facebook (zubainkubwa) for all the latest product updates!

### Q: Is my theme preference synced across devices?
**A**: No, each device stores its own preference. You can have different themes on your phone and laptop.

### Q: Does the website work without JavaScript?
**A**: The website works great! However, automatic theme switching requires JavaScript. You can still access all content.

### Q: Is the site accessible for people with disabilities?
**A**: Yes! The site is designed with accessibility in mind. Both light and dark modes have good contrast. Use keyboard navigation or screen readers if needed.
